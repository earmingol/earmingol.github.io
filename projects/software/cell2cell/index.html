Computational suite for studying cell-cell interactions and communication

The suite cell2cell includes two main sub-tools:

  • The regular cell2cell, which includes the main approaches described in our review.
  • Tensor-cell2cell, a novel method that uses tensor factorization to deconvolve patterns of cell-cell communication across multiple cellular contexts (e.g., time points, disease states, cellular location, patients, etc.), as described in our research article.
